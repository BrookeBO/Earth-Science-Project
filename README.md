# Earth-Science-Project
Spring 2026 Northwestern earth science project documentation
PFAs in water: Is there an observable upward trend in PFAs in water? Where is there the most/which locations are impacted most by PFAs in water? 
PFAs pose many risks to people such ask increased chances of obesity, cancers, reduced immune system ability in fighting infections, and can make impacts on women's fertility.
Drinking water contaminated by PFAs is the most common way which people consume PFAs, which makes the concentration in drinking water very important. 
Sources and data are in 02_RawData
For the location approach, I plan to make graphs of PFA concentration vs time to see if there are season fluxuations, and a trend over several years. Then, I will use concentration maps to see which areas have the greatest concentration, and look for similarities in the areas which could impact PFAs to see what may be causing these trends.
