# Earth-Science-Project
Spring 2026 Northwestern earth science project documentation
PFAs in water: Is there an observable upward trend in PFAs in water? Where is there the most/which locations are impacted most by PFAs in water? 
PFAs pose many risks to people. Increased chances of obesity, cancers, reduces the immune system's ability to fight infections, and impacts women's fertility.
Drinking water contaminated by PFAs is the most common way which people consume PFAs, which makes the concentration in drinking water very important.
